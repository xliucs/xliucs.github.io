Xin Liu

Xin Liu is a Senior Research Scientist at Google Research. He received his PhD in Computer Science from the University of Washington in 2023, where he was advised by Shwetak Patel and Daniel McDuff. His PhD research was supported by the Google PhD Fellowship .

Before joining UW, he obtained his bachelorâ€™s degree in computer science from the University of Massachusetts Amherst in 2018, where he started his research with Sunghoon Ivan Lee .

His work is at the intersection of AI for health and science, LLM reasoning, and machine learning. He is interested in building a unified AGI system that benefits for social good.
